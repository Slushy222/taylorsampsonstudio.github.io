about

Taylor Sampson is an American artist, designer, photographer, developer, and storyteller based in Chattanooga, Tennessee. His work seeks to explore the confrontations of the self and environment while examining systemic issues. He is earning his degree in BFA Graphic Design as an Undergraduate at the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga.
